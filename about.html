Our Story

Started in 2025 by Vani Tomar, Vani’s Medicos began as a small neighborhood pharmacy in Noida with one simple goal: make quality healthcare and personal‑care products accessible and affordable for everyone. Today we serve thousands of happy customers across India through our online store and doorstep delivery.

Mission

To deliver authentic, affordable medicines and cosmetics with unmatched customer care.

Vision

To become India’s most trusted e‑commerce brand for health, beauty, and wellness products.
